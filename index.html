Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a mobile barber appointment in Rocky Mount, Greenville, Wilson, or Tarboro?

Booking your at-home haircut is easy! Use our online booking system, fill out the contact form on this page, or call/text James directly at (919) 809-0529. We serve Rocky Mount, Greenville, Wilson, and Tarboro NC. For same-day appointments, calling or texting is the fastest option.

What mobile barber services do you offer?

We offer a full range of men's grooming services delivered to your door: precision haircuts, skin fades, low taper fades, mid fades, high fades, kids haircuts, beard trims, beard shaping, and executive grooming packages. All services include professional-grade tools and products for barbershop-quality results at your home or office.

Can I cancel or reschedule my appointment?

While we accept walk-ins, we highly recommend booking an appointment in advance to ensure your preferred time slot and barber are available.

What areas in Eastern NC do you serve as a mobile barber?

Our mobile barber service covers Rocky Mount NC, Greenville NC, Wilson NC, and Tarboro NC. We operate Monday through Sunday with different service areas on different days. Contact us to find out when we'll be in your area or to schedule a convenient appointment time.

Do you offer discounts for group bookings or events?

Yes, we offer haircuts for children. Our skilled barbers are experienced in providing stylish and comfortable cuts for kids of all ages.

What types of payment do you accept?

We accept cash, credit cards, and debit cards. For your convenience, contactless payment options such as Apple Pay and Google Pay are also available.

How far in advance should I book?

We ask for at least 24 hours' notice if you need to reschedule. Since James travels to you, last-minute cancellations significantly impact our travel schedule and ability to serve other clients on the route.

Do you charge travel fees?

Travel costs are already included within our standard service pricing for Greenville, Rocky Mount, Wilson, and Tarboro. If you are outside these areas, please contact us for a custom quote.
