Our Mobile Services

Precision Haircut

Whether you want a classic cut, modern style, or sharp taper fade, every haircut is delivered with precision right at your location. Using industry-leading tools, James ensures every detail is on point, from the lineup to the finishing touches. 30 minutes of dedicated, one-on-one grooming.

Skin Fade

Our signature skin fade is smooth, perfectly blended, and finished with crisp razor edging. From low to high fades, every transition is seamless. This is the go-to service for professionals who demand a sharp, clean look without the wait at a traditional shop. 40 minutes of expert craftsmanship.

Haircut & Beard Combo

The complete package for the modern gentleman. A precision haircut paired with a full beard trim, shape, and sculpt, all lined up with a straight razor for that razor-sharp finish. Sanitized tools, premium products, and 45 minutes of dedicated attention to detail.

Kid's Haircut

A patient, professional, and fun grooming experience for the young ones. We bring the full mobile setup right to your home, making it easy and stress-free for both kids and parents. Clean tools, great communication, and a fresh cut in just 30 minutes.

Beard Grooming & Conditioning

Keep your beard looking sharp between full appointments. A detailed beard trim, precise shaping, and deep conditioning treatment that leaves your facial hair soft, healthy, and perfectly maintained. 15 minutes of focused care with premium products.

Full Service Executive Package

The ultimate mobile grooming experience. Includes a custom precision cut, professional styling, a sculpted and conditioned beard trim, and a hot towel finish. Everything you need to look your absolute best, delivered directly to your doorstep or office.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book an appointment with Mobile Barber LLC?

You can book an appointment directly through our website contact form, or by calling/texting James at the number provided. Since we are mobile, we recommend booking in advance to ensure your location is within our daily route.

What services do you offer?

James offers premium precision haircuts, skin fades, beard grooming, and executive packages. Every service is brought directly to your location with all the professional tools of a high-end barbershop.

Can I cancel or reschedule my appointment?

While we accept walk-ins, we highly recommend booking an appointment in advance to ensure your preferred time slot and barber are available.

What areas do you provide mobile barber services in?

We generally operate Monday through Saturday, covering specific regions on different days. Please contact us to see when James is scheduled to be in your area (Greenville, Rocky Mount, Wilson, or Tarboro).

Do you offer discounts for group bookings or events?

Yes, we offer haircuts for children. Our skilled barbers are experienced in providing stylish and comfortable cuts for kids of all ages.

What types of payment do you accept?

We accept cash, credit cards, and debit cards. For your convenience, contactless payment options such as Apple Pay and Google Pay are also available.

How far in advance should I book?

We ask for at least 24 hours' notice if you need to reschedule. Since James travels to you, last-minute cancellations significantly impact our travel schedule and ability to serve other clients on the route.

Do you charge travel fees?

Travel costs are already included within our standard service pricing for Greenville, Rocky Mount, Wilson, and Tarboro. If you are outside these areas, please contact us for a custom quote.
